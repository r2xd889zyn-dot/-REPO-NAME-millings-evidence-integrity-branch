# Proof of Truth™ — Millings Method

## Overview

The Millings Layering Method™ is a dual-spine architecture that:

1. Controls what is allowed to happen (Control Spine)
2. Proves what has happened (Evidence Spine)

---

## The Problem

Most systems can:

- Execute actions
- Produce outputs
- Log events

But they cannot:

- Prove outputs were unchanged
- Demonstrate continuity over time
- Provide independently verifiable evidence
- Present results in admissible form

---

## The Solution

The Millings Method™ separates:

Control → Execution → Evidence

---

## Control Spine

Ensures only valid states are allowed to exist and act.

- SAL™ → Signal admissibility
- MMCT™ → Structural validity
- AATP™ → Authorship traceability
- Execution Gate → Action control

---

## Evidence Spine

Ensures outputs can be proven after execution.

- MCCL™ → Continuity preservation
- Verification Script → Independent validation
- Admissibility Template → Legal readiness
- Evidence Package → End-to-end proof chain

---

## What This Enables

A system that can:

- Prevent invalid actions
- Preserve valid outputs
- Reconstruct history
- Prove outcomes externally

---

## Core Principle

A system is not trustworthy because it runs.

A system is trustworthy because its outputs can be:

- Verified
- Reproduced
- Proven

---

## Canonical Statement

The Millings Method™ does not only control reality.

It proves reality.
